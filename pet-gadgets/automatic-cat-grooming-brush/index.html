Automatic Cat Grooming Brush

Published: June 7, 2026

Quick answer: Automatic cat grooming brushes are corner or wall-mounted arch brushes that cats rub against to groom themselves — no human involvement required, and most cats love them.

How they work

The brush is a flexible arch with bristles on the inside. You mount it at cat head height in a wall corner or on a doorframe. Cats naturally rub their head, cheeks, and body against it to scratch, groom, and distribute natural oils through their coat. Most cats discover and use them within a day or two.

Benefits

Reduces shedding by removing loose fur before it falls, satisfies the natural urge to rub against textured surfaces, and helps distribute skin oils for a healthier coat. Particularly useful for cats that resist being brushed by hand — they control the interaction entirely.

Catnip tip

Most models include a small compartment for catnip near the bristles. Fill it when first installing to attract your cat to the brush. Once the habit is established, cats use it without needing catnip as an incentive.

Final thoughts

One of the easiest grooming solutions available. Installation takes minutes, most cats adopt it within days, and it works in addition to manual brushing rather than replacing it.
