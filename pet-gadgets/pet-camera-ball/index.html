Pet Camera Ball: Remote Play For Your Pet

Published: May 19, 2026

Quick answer: A pet camera ball is an app-controlled rolling device with a built-in camera that lets you play with and watch your pet from anywhere.

How it works

The ball connects to your home Wi-Fi. You control its direction and speed remotely through an app, while the onboard camera streams live video so you can see how your pet reacts. Most models move in all directions on smooth surfaces.

Best for which pets

Most effective for cats and small dogs with a strong prey drive — they'll chase it naturally. Large or powerful dogs may be too rough and damage the ball quickly. Works best on hard floors; carpets can slow or stop the motor entirely.

Practical limitations

Battery life is typically 30 to 60 minutes per charge. Video quality is limited by the small onboard lens. Wi-Fi connectivity can drop when the ball rolls into a different room or behind furniture. Expect some connectivity and durability trade-offs at most price points.

Final thoughts

A fun way to interact with your pet remotely. Set realistic expectations for battery life and surface compatibility before buying.
