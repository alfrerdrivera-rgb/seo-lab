Pet Camera And Treat Dispenser: Complete Guide

Published: May 17, 2026

Quick answer: A pet camera with treat dispenser lets you monitor your pet via live video, speak to them through a built-in speaker, and launch treats on command from your phone.

How it works

The device connects to your home Wi-Fi and streams live video to your phone. Most models include two-way audio, night vision, motion alerts, and a treat launcher that shoots small round treats across the room when you tap a button in the app.

What to look for

Video quality (1080p minimum), treat launch range and accuracy, treat size compatibility (most require small round treats like Temptations for cats or similar for dogs), app reliability, and encryption for the camera feed. Some models also include sound or motion detection alerts.

Introducing it to your pet

Not all pets respond well to the treat launcher noise at first. Some find the sound startling and may avoid the device. Introduce it gradually by placing treats near it manually first, so your pet builds a positive association before the launcher activates.

Final thoughts

Great for managing separation anxiety and keeping pets engaged. Test treat compatibility and app stability before relying on it when traveling.
