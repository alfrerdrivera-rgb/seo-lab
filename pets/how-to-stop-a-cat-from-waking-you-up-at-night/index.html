How To Stop A Cat From Waking You Up At Night

Quick answer: Cats waking you up at night is common but fixable. They often do it for attention, food, or habit. You need to reset their routine.

Why this happens

Cats are active at night by nature. If they have excess energy, they’ll use you as entertainment. Tip: play with your cat for 10–15 minutes before bedtime to tire them out.

What you can do

Feeding schedules matter more than you think. If they associate you waking up with food, they’ll keep doing it. Tip: use an automatic feeder to break that connection.

When to worry

Attention reinforces the behavior. If you react, even negatively, they win. Tip: ignore nighttime disturbances completely for a few days to reset the habit.

Final thoughts

Consistency is key. It may take a few nights of struggle, but once the pattern breaks, your sleep improves fast. Stay disciplined.
